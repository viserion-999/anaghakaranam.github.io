Where and when would a food delivery app display their ad on a famous tv series website?¶

PROGRAM EVALUATION: Do millennial Binge watchers order food online?¶

What is the fuss all about?¶

This idea began when I was binge watching a South Indian Web Series on a famous website. For every few minutes there was an ad being displayed of a food delivery app. What struck me is how were they deciding of the ad is being displayed to the right audience? Please pay attention to the questions below. What other questions can you think of?

  • What if the ads are being displayed to people who do not eat outside food? Isnt it waste of investment on ads?
  • Do people who watch series for a long duration click on the ad and order food? (Binge Watchers) In other words, is there a correlation between bingewatching and ordering food online?(One of those millennial bad habits)
  • What browser are viewers using? Is it also related to the above question by any chance?
  • What month would displaying the ad be most effective? Example: Summer and Winter vacations are the time millinnials snuggle to binge watch and order food online.
  • Based on this information, the website owners can charge different amounts to advertisers? An amazing strategy to get sponsors too.

Imagine there is a desert delivery app entering the market and wants to target millinnials. Especially couch potatoes.(Ouch!) So, they decide to sponsor a webseries and approach a famous company currently hosting an Indian TV Series who are currently looking for sponsors.

The manager of the delivery app has to decide if they should place their ad and sponsor this TV series.¶

Because of their cost restrictions, the company can only afford to sponsor in one location in India, and they want to take maximum benefit.

The manager has the following datasets for about 90k users:

  • users.csv - Information about users registered in 2015 & 2016, in different cities.
  • engagement.csv - Which browser and how many hours per week is each user watching the series. And, also if the user is binge watching or drip watching
  • behavior.csv - Whether the user clicks on the ad and if the user is ordering food.

Note: We are assuming that the user_id is same on the food delivery app and the tv series website. (Would be realistic if it was email id, but for convenience we use numbers.

We are going to help the manager by predicting the following:

1. In which city he should place his ad?¶

2. We check if people are watching the tv series for a long duration, do they click? Do they order food? If this is true, should the manager increase the frequency of ads as the number of hours per week user watches increases?¶

3. Which season/month is best to place the ad?¶

4. Is there any other factor affecting the correlation between clicking on ads, and ordering/not ordering food? Example: Browser?¶

Why it is still okay to have a low AUROC and good accuracy score: Check out these links:

  • https://stackoverflow.com/questions/47104129/getting-a-low-roc-auc-score-but-a-high-accuracy
  • https://stats.stackexchange.com/questions/308513/roc-auc-is-higher-than-accuracy-score-what-does-this-mean

What it essentially means is: We need not care about low AUROC when we are able to get good accuracy value. Because AUROC you get (something like) the accuracy averaged across all possible thresholds and what this classifier does in a purely theoretical and abstract situation when averaged across all possible thresholds should pose very little interest for a practitioner. What is of real importance is confusion matrix, precision, recall, fscore & accuracy.

Business Conclusions:¶

Our final conclusions are completely different from the trends we noticed before with single variables.

  1. Month of displaying the ad is probably related to ordering food. This is a fascinating discovery since we assume people who binge watch, order food.
  2. Also, people watching TV Series between the range of 3 to 6 hours usually order food. Another unexpected result. So probably, the frequency of ad should be more for these viewers.
  3. Bengaluru is the most important feature followed by Hyderabad.

Again, these still cannot be concluded. The Manager could run a sample program with a focus group using these results for a few days before investing.
